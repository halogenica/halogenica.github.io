Custom Keyboard - DZ65RGB

 Posted on June 28, 2019

My previous TADA68 build mysteriously bit the dust, so I decided to build a new keyboard and reuse my TADA68 aluminum case. Thankfully I found a fantastic (nearly) drop-in repalcement PCB: the DZ65RGB. It is another 65% keyboard like the TADA68, and has my most desired improvements over the TADA68: individually addressible RGB LEDs, USB-C, hot-swappable switches, and a lovely purple and gold color scheme!
